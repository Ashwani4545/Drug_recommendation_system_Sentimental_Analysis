About the project

Built to make drug reviews
clearer, safer, and easier

DrugSense is a Flask web app for exploring patient drug reviews with sentiment analysis, aspect scoring, keyword highlighting, and simple condition-based recommendations.

What this app does

01

Analyzes review sentiment

Reviews are classified as positive or negative with a confidence score, and the result is shown in a simple visual summary.

02

Breaks reviews into aspects

The app looks at effectiveness, side effects, dosage convenience, and cost so the result is easier to interpret.

03

Surfaces drug suggestions

When the review mentions a condition, DrugSense can show commonly reviewed medication alternatives for that area.

04

Highlights key words

Positive and negative words are highlighted so you can see why the prediction leaned in a certain direction.

Project notes Informational use only
This project is intended for learning and demonstration. It should not replace a medical professional’s advice.
