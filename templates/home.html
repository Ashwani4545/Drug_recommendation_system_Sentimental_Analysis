How it works

01

Sentiment classification

An RNN model trained on 200,000+ drug reviews predicts overall positive or negative sentiment with a confidence score.

02

Aspect-based analysis

We decompose the review into four medical aspects — effectiveness, side effects, dosage convenience, and cost — each scored independently.

03
